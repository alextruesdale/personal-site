Digital Business & Working Internationally

Complex and constantly expanding, digital business and its impact on the accessiblity of a global career fascinates me. To gain first-hand experience in this international environment, I moved to Berlin, Germany, interning at a digital marketing agency and trying my hand at living abroad.

Why Move to Germany?

The value of international experience became clear as my interests grew beyond the borders of the United States. A key step to building a unique career, life in Berlin was a rich and diverse experience, working with colleagues from all over the world and laying the foundation for further ambitions at home and abroad.

Creative Pursuits

Outside of professional life, I find myself drawn to photography and design.

I like to test my skills in web and graphic design, creating digital illustrations or building webpages — like this one. Conversely, when it comes to photography, I shoot exclusively on film and with analogue cameras, both 35mm and middle format. The live-medium of film creates a uniquely fallible experience and often produces interesting and unexpected results.
